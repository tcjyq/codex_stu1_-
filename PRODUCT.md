# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

This personal homepage is for internship mentors, recruiters, classmates, and collaborators who need a quick but memorable view of Ma Hongjia's direction, abilities, projects, and contact information.

## Product Purpose

The site presents a polished personal identity around AI large-model applications and data analysis. Success means visitors quickly understand the user's learning direction, trust the attention to detail, and know how to contact him.

## Brand Personality

Dark, precise, fluid. The page should feel like a calm technical portfolio with premium motion rather than a generic resume template.

## Anti-references

Avoid inactive decorative buttons, crowded hero layout, generic card grids, broken Chinese encoding, over-bright ripples, and effects that hide the starfield background.

## Design Principles

- Keep the starfield and mouse gravity as the recognizable signature.
- Let motion feel physical: slow orbiting, liquid drift, soft magnetic response.
- Give the hero more breathing room so the first fold feels confident, not cramped.
- Preserve readability and clear contact paths before adding decoration.
- Use dark minimal surfaces with liquid-glass depth, not heavy template glassmorphism.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect reduced-motion preferences. Keep Chinese text legible on dark backgrounds with strong contrast, and preserve keyboard-friendly native links and file upload behavior.
